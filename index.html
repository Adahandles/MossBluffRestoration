Project Overview

The Moss Bluff Restoration Project aims to restore and enhance the wetland ecosystem on the Moss Bluff property in Florida. The project will mitigate flooding, improve water quality, restore native vegetation, and provide educational and recreational opportunities. The restoration efforts are guided by research, local knowledge, and collaboration with agencies and stakeholders.
